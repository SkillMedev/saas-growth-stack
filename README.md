# SaaS Growth Stack

**Acquisition, activation, retention — the full SaaS growth toolkit.** — built in-house by [Skill&nbsp;Me](https://skillme.dev).

Everything you need to grow a SaaS product. Covers growth modeling, churn reduction, expansion revenue, customer success QBRs, product analytics, and unit economics.

⭐ **If this is useful, star the repo** — it's how we gauge what to build next.

## Install

- **From the catalog:** [skillme.dev/pack/saas-growth-stack](https://skillme.dev/pack/saas-growth-stack) — install the whole pack into Claude in one step.
- **With the skills CLI:** `npx skills add aouellets/saas-growth-stack`
- **Manually:** copy any `skills/<slug>/SKILL.md` into your Claude skills directory.

## Skills in this pack

- **[Growth Model](skills/growth-model/SKILL.md)** — Builds a growth model with acquisition loops, activation rates, and compounding projections.
- **[Churn Reduction](skills/churn-reduction/SKILL.md)** — Diagnoses churn root causes and designs intervention playbooks by segment.
- **[Expansion Revenue](skills/expansion-revenue/SKILL.md)** — Designs upsell and cross-sell playbooks with triggers, talk tracks, and success metrics.
- **[Customer Success QBR](skills/customer-success-qbr/SKILL.md)** — Designs quarterly business reviews — value delivered, health score, expansion conversation.
- **[Product Analytics](skills/product-analytics/SKILL.md)** — Sets up product analytics: event taxonomy, funnel tracking, cohort analysis.
- **[Unit Economics](skills/unit-economics/SKILL.md)** — Calculates and optimizes CAC, LTV, payback, and contribution margin.
- **[SaaS Pricing Model](skills/saas-pricing/SKILL.md)** — Designs SaaS pricing tiers with value metric, packaging, and expansion revenue structure.
